How Will Society Withstand the AI Revolution?

Large-scale artificial intelligence has begun to confront humanity with a historic challenge: how will we deal with a technology that is designed to surpass the boundaries of human understanding and control? The solution to these challenges lies in resilience: as a society, we must develop the ability to sustainably adapt to unexpected challenges in AI.

The Challenge of Large-Scale AI

There are several fundamental challenges posed by large-scale AI:

  • How to handle generative models that can fool human perception and shape human behavior.
  • How to harness models trained think in novel ways unseen in all recorded human knowledge.

The solution to these challenges does not lie in the technology alone, but in a societal investment in the science, engineering, and culture of resilience around AI.

Resilience and Interpretability

To develop a resilient AI ecosystem, we must invest in three things:

  1. The science of understanding AI so that humans can know why AI goes wrong when it does and what should be fixed.
  2. The technical practice of control of AI so humans have the ability to fix the things that need fixing.
  3. A culture of power over AI so that humans have the right, responsibility, and expectation of controlling AI.
