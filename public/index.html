How Will Society Withstand the AI Revolution?

Large-scale artificial intelligence has begun to confront humanity with a historic challenge: how will we deal with a technology that is designed to surpass the boundaries of human understanding and control? How will we handle generative models that can fool human perception and shape human behavior? How can we harness reasoning models trained to develop new thinking patterns intended to surpass all recorded human knowledge?

The solution to these challenges lies in resilience: as a society, we must develop the ability to continually adapt to unexpected capabilities in AI. This entails investment in three things. Developing the science of understanding AI so that humans can know why AI behaves as it does and what should be fixed. Developing the technical practice of control of AI so humans have the ability to fix the things that need fixing. And and developing a culture of power over AI so that humans have the right, responsibility, and expectation of controlling AI.
